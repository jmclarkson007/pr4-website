Business & Partnership

PR4Shopping is a profit-making sole trader specialising in business services. We bridge the gap between transport infrastructure and local commercial success.
Founding members pay a one-off fee of £1.00. Businesses joining six months after our launch will pay a £19.99 setup fee.
The first year is free. After that, your fee is based on your actual results over a year based on a set spending cap. Each business has their own rate and spending cap which will be shown when you sign-up on the app. If you get zero redemptions, you pay nothing. If you get results we will send you the number of customers you gave redemptions to using our app, and base the price on that figure going forward as a monthly payment. You can cancel at any time.
