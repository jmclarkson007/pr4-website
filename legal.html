Data Protection & Privacy

PR4Shopping operates under a strict "No-Collection" data policy for individual users. Our protocols ensure:

  • Business Data Only: In accordance with GDPR and the Data Protection Act, we only collect and process data related to Business entities (Merchant accounts).
  • No Personal Data: We do not collect or store personal data from shoppers or casual visitors.
  • No Cookies: This site and the PR4Shopping application do not use cookies.
  • No IP Tracking: We do not collect or log IP addresses of our users.

Accessibility Statement

We believe local shopping should be accessible to everyone. The PR4Shopping application is built to meet WCAG 2.1 AA compliance standards, including:

  • Full screen-reader compatibility.
  • High-contrast text and logical heading structures.
  • Keyboard-navigable interface for all core features.
