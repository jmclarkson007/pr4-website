PR4Shopping operates with a "Privacy by Design" approach. Our data collection is strictly limited to the following:

  • Business Data Only: Under GDPR and the Data Protection Act, we only collect and process business-related information (Merchant names, business addresses, and commercial contact details).
  • No Personal Data: We do not collect, store, or solicit personal data from individual shoppers.
  • No IP Addresses: We do not log or track visitor IP addresses.
  • No Cookies: This website and the PR4Shopping app do not use cookies for tracking, marketing, or any other purpose.
