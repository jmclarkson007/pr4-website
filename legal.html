Data Protection & Privacy

PR4Shopping operates under a strict "Privacy by Design" framework. Our data protocols ensure:

  • Business Data Only: Under GDPR and the Data Protection Act, we only collect and process information related to Business entities (Merchant accounts).
  • No Personal Data: We do not collect, store, or solicit personal data from individual shoppers.
  • No Cookies: This website and the PR4Shopping application do not use cookies for tracking or any other purpose.
  • No IP Addresses: We do not log or track visitor IP addresses.

Accessibility Statement

We are committed to digital inclusivity. The PR4Shopping application is designed to meet WCAG 2.1 AA compliance standards, including:
