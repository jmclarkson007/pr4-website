Building Your Town Centre
Using the Park & Ride

Strategic economic integration designed to divert footfall from out-of-town malls back to the High Street.

The Economic Bridge

PR4Shopping is more than an app; it is a strategic intervention for Local Authorities and BIDs. We use existing sustainable transport infrastructure to create a high-intent shopper pipeline, ensuring that every bus journey directly fuels local retail resilience.

Urban Vitality

By rewarding Park & Ride use with local value, we increase transit yield while reducing city-centre congestion. This protects the historic core while supporting modern commerce.

Retail Resilience

We provide local shops with a "VIP entrance" for shoppers. Exclusive P&R discounts give High Street stores a competitive edge over out-of-town retail parks.

Sustainable Growth

Motorists see the cost of travel offset by immediate savings. This turns environmental policy into a positive consumer benefit, driving voluntary compliance.

Framework for Success

1
Zero-Capital Deployment: We provide the digital bridge and merchant onboarding. Local Authorities provide the reach through existing terminal and transit signage.
2
Verified Geolocation: Incentives are only activated for verified Park & Ride users, ensuring the scheme supports your transport and environmental goals.
3
Economic Intelligence: Gain annual data insights on shopper movement and scheme impact, aiding future urban planning and ESG reporting.
4
2026 National Rollout: We are currently selecting 12 Lead UK Cities to pioneer this integrated approach to town centre regeneration.
