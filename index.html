The Triple Win Strategy

PR4Shopping isn't just an app; it's a behavioral intervention designed to balance the needs of the Council, the Retailer, and the Motorist.

For Councils

Increases Park & Ride yield and reduces inner-city congestion. Provides tangible proof of support for the local High Street without drawing from public budgets.

For Retailers

Delivers high-intent shoppers directly from the city perimeter. Exclusive P&R-only discounts ensure footfall is diverted from out-of-town malls back to the core.

For Motorists

Offers a "VIP Entrance" to the city. The cost of the bus ticket is offset by immediate, exclusive savings at their favorite local brands.

Operational Pillars

1
Zero-Cost Implementation: We provide the digital infrastructure and retailer onboarding. The Council simply provides the "Bridge" via terminal and on-bus signage.
2
Dynamic Incentives: Discounts are only active when validated via P&R terminals, ensuring the scheme directly supports your sustainable transport goals.
3
Data Insights: Councils receive quarterly reports on footfall patterns and "Modal Shift" success rates, aiding future planning and ESG reporting.
4
Regional Allocation: We are currently assigning 12 UK Lead Cities to the 2026 rollout, ensuring regional exclusivity and dedicated support.
